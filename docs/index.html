Hi, I'm Toufiq.

I am an applied microeconomist. My research applies rigorous quasi-experimental and machine learning methods to large datasets to measure the causal effects of economic policies and incentives. My work has examined the impact of performance-based teacher bonuses, the role of AP exam fee waivers on college-credit attainment, and the health consequences of public insurance contractions.

I am currently a Research Assistant (Postdoc) at the Johns Hopkins Carey Business School. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from Georgia State University.
