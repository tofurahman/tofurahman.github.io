Hi, I'm Toufiq.

I am an applied microeconomist specializing in health, education, and labor economics. My research employs modern econometric and machine learning methods to analyze the real-world impacts of policy interventions, such as the effects of teacher incentives on student outcomes and the consequences of Medicaid changes on public health.

I am currently a Postdoc at the Johns Hopkins Carey Business School. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from Georgia State University.
